    System Transparency FAQs

    Are the report metrics based on live capital?
    Unless clearly labeled otherwise, the metrics shown on this page represent simulated data from our paper trading sandbox. They are intended for reviewing how the automated logic performs under defined rules, not as a guarantee of live market results.
    How does live automation differ from paper mode?
    Paper mode assumes perfect fills to help you test your logic safely. Live automation (unlocked via XeanVI PRO) executes in the real market, meaning it faces actual liquidity constraints, spread variations, and potential slippage that can alter outcomes.
    How does XeanVI use AI-assisted market scoring?
    Before execution, XeanVI PRO uses AI-assisted scoring to validate market context, headlines, and catalyst language against your playbook. These scores are software-generated workflow signals, not financial advice or guaranteed trade predictors.
    Does XeanVI hold my funds?
    No. XeanVI routes execution commands to your supported broker via API. We do not hold, transfer, or manage your capital. You maintain full control over your broker account and permissions.
