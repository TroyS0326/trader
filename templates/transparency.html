The AI Decision Pipeline

1. Raw Data Ingestion

The scanner aggregates raw, unfiltered data from multiple financial exchanges and news wires simultaneously. It filters out stocks with low liquidity to ensure safe entries and exits.

Alpaca SIP Data Feed SEC Filings (EDGAR) Benzinga Pro News API Float/Short Interest Scrapers
2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Volume without a reason is a trap. The AI reads the morning news for every gapping stock and assigns a Catalyst Score (1-100) based on the strength of the fundamental news.

[+] HIGH IMPACT: FDA Approvals, Earnings Beats, Government Contracts
[-] LOW IMPACT: Chatroom hype, unsubstantiated rumors, secondary offerings
3. Technical & Risk Alignment

A great catalyst still needs a great chart. The engine calculates the mathematical Risk-to-Reward ratio based on support/resistance lines. If the stock passes both NLP and Technical checks, it is graded an A+ Setup.
