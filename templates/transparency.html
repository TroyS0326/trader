Unless clearly labeled otherwise, the metrics shown on this page should be treated as paper trading or simulated workflow data. Paper trading can help users review strategy behavior, but it does not guarantee live results.

XeanVI may use AI-assisted scoring to help organize market context, news language, and catalyst information. These scores are software-generated workflow signals and should not be treated as financial advice or investment recommendations.
