Automated Trade Journaling

Manual journaling is where discipline dies. XeanVI automatically logs every entry, stop, and target hit to your local SQLite database.

  • Context Retention: Every trade is linked to the original scan data, catalyst scores, and market internals at the moment of entry.
  • Managed Execution History: Tracks peg-limit fills and automated scale-outs to analyze slippage and fill quality.
  • Privacy-First Storage: Your data remains local in veteran_trades.db, ensuring your proprietary edge is never uploaded to a cloud server.
