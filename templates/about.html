About XeanVI

XeanVI is a trading workflow automation software platform. Users define playbook rules, risk settings, and broker permissions, and XeanVI executes only within those configured controls.

What XeanVI does

  • Provides workflow automation tools for scanning, setup validation, and broker-connected routing when enabled.
  • Supports paper testing so users can review behavior before live use.
  • Provides transparency documentation about safeguards and limitations.

What XeanVI does not do

  • Does not provide investment, financial, legal, or tax advice.
  • Does not remove trading risk; users remain responsible for trading decisions and broker permissions.
  • Does not guarantee outcomes or performance.

Editorial and AI Assistance

Public blog and educational materials may be drafted or assisted by internal tooling and AI workflows, then reviewed for product alignment. Content is intended for product education and workflow explanation, not financial advice. Users remain responsible for their own trading decisions.
