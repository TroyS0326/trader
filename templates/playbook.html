Under the Hood

Transparency is our core feature. Here is exactly how the XeanVI algorithm scans the market, scores catalysts, and executes risk-managed trades on your behalf.

1

The Morning Scan

Between 9:00 AM and 9:30 AM EST, our scanner sifts through thousands of tickers. We aren't looking for random spikes; we require a structural setup.

  • Relative Volume (RVOL): Must be exceptionally high to ensure liquidity.
  • Gap %: The stock must be gapping up or down significantly pre-market.
  • AI Catalyst Scoring: We read the morning news via AI to ensure the volume is driven by real events (earnings, FDA approvals, contracts), not just chatroom hype.
2

Precision Entry

We never chase. If a setup receives an A or A+ Grade, the bot waits for the market to open and confirm the trend.

Once the "Buy Window" opens, we calculate maximum position size based on your exact Bankroll and Risk Settings to ensure you never lose more than a fixed dollar amount on a single bad trade.

3

Risk Management Automation

Once in a trade, human emotion is removed. The system immediately places a hard stop-loss and a two-tier profit bracket.

Target 1 (50% Sale): Secures base profit.

Breakeven Stop: Once Target 1 hits, the stop-loss on the remaining shares moves to your entry price. You now have a risk-free trade.

Target 2 (Runner): A trailing stop catches massive moves, selling automatically when the trend bends.

System Limitations & Realities

No algorithm wins 100% of the time. The XeanVI playbook is designed for "base hits" and strict capital preservation. Expect losing streaks, choppy market days where the scanner returns "NO TRADE," and occasional slippage during high-volatility events. Our system is built on mathematical discipline, not guaranteed wealth. We strongly advise running the bot in Paper Trading mode for at least two weeks before committing real capital.
