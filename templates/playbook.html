The Bot's Playbook

The bot doesn't guess. It follows a strict, 3-step disciplined sequence to find and execute trades automatically.

1

Step 1: Automated Screening

The bot starts every morning by filtering the entire market for high-probability setups using specific mathematical triggers:

  • Catalyst Discovery: AI-based scoring of news headlines.
  • RVOL & Liquidity: Identifying stocks with massive institutional volume.
  • Float Proxy: Filtering for stocks with the right "supply" for momentum moves.
2

Step 2: Logic Validation

Before a single dollar is risked, the bot validates the setup against your technical rules:

  • Daily Alignment: Ensuring the intraday move matches the long-term trend.
  • Relative Strength: Comparing the stock against market benchmarks.
  • Tape Proxies: Real-time monitoring of price action and momentum.
3

Step 3: Automated Execution

Once validated, the bot takes over the manual clicking. It manages the trade from entry to exit:

  • Pegged Entry: Automated limit orders to secure the best price.
  • Defined Risk: Hard stops placed immediately at the broker level.
  • Scale-Outs: Automatically takes profit at your pre-defined targets.
