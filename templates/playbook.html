Playbook Automation FAQs

What exactly is the Trading Playbook?
The Playbook is the central logic engine of XeanVI. It is where you configure the specific market criteria, risk percentages, and order parameters that the PRO software will follow when scanning and routing trades.
Can I customize my entry and exit targets?
Yes. You have full control over establishing target zones, stop-loss percentages, and scale-out logic. XeanVI PRO uses these rules to attach automated bracket orders when executing a trade.
Do I have to know how to code?
No coding is required. The Playbook features a visual, form-based interface that allows you to easily input and adjust your technical rules, which the backend engine translates into automation logic.
Can I test my rules before live trading?
Absolutely. We strongly encourage all users to test their customized playbook in the paper trading sandbox first to observe how the automation behaves before connecting a live PRO execution workflow.
