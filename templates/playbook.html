Trading Playbook for Rule-Based Strategy Automation

The XeanVI Trading Playbook helps you define setup criteria, risk settings, paper trading workflows, and broker automation rules before using live execution tools.

Explore the Workflow View Features

Why a Trading Playbook Matters

A trading playbook turns scattered ideas into a repeatable process. Instead of reacting to every chart, headline, or price move, you can define the conditions you want to review before a setup becomes actionable.

XeanVI uses your playbook settings to support a more structured workflow across market scans, paper trading tests, risk controls, and supported broker connection tools.

The XeanVI Playbook Workflow

A step-by-step look at how playbook rules can support market review, validation, and execution workflows.

01
PHASE_01 // MARKET REVIEW
Rule-Based Market Screening

XeanVI helps organize market activity using configurable filters and AI-assisted context. The goal is to reduce scattered manual review and bring possible setups into a more structured workflow.

  • NLP
    News and Catalyst Context: Use AI-assisted market scoring to review headlines, filings, and catalyst language alongside your own playbook rules.
  • VOL
    Volume and Liquidity Filters: Review assets against relative volume, liquidity, and activity thresholds that fit your preferred strategy style.
  • FLT
    Float and Volatility Context: Add float-related and volatility-related filters to help organize which setups deserve further review.
02
PHASE_02 // RULE CHECK
Strategy and Risk Validation

Before a workflow moves forward, your playbook settings help compare a setup against your selected risk, timing, and market-condition preferences.

  • RISK
    Risk Settings: Define position sizing, maximum risk, stop settings, and review limits so each workflow follows the structure you set in advance.
  • MKT
    Market Context: Compare potential setups against broader symbols, indexes, trend conditions, and rule-based confirmation settings.
  • TEST
    Paper Trading Review: Use simulated workflows to observe how your rules behave before deciding whether to connect supported live broker tools.
03
PHASE_03 // EXECUTION WORKFLOW
Broker-Connected Automation Settings

When enabled by the user, XeanVI can support broker-connected workflows through supported API integrations. Users remain responsible for reviewing settings, risk, permissions, and trade activity.

  • ORD
    Order Workflow Settings: Configure entry preferences, order types, and routing behavior through supported broker integration tools.
  • STP
    Stop and Exit Rules: Define stop settings and exit logic so each workflow follows the risk parameters selected in your playbook.
  • TGT
    Target and Scale Settings: Set target zones, partial exit preferences, and review rules for managing structured trade workflows.

Trading Playbook FAQs

Why do I need a Trading Playbook?
A Trading Playbook helps you define your rules before market pressure begins. It gives your workflow structure around setup criteria, risk settings, paper trading tests, and execution preferences.
Can I customize the strategy and execution parameters?
Yes. XeanVI is designed to let users configure risk percentages, volume filters, setup criteria, stop settings, target rules, and supported broker automation preferences from the dashboard.
Do I have to code my own strategy?
No coding is required to use the Trading Playbook. XeanVI is designed around visual settings and configurable rules so users can build structured workflows without writing code.
Does XeanVI guarantee trading results?
No. XeanVI provides trading workflow software, paper trading tools, market review features, and supported broker connectivity. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or guaranteed trading results.
