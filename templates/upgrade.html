Pricing & Billing Details

What does PRO include?

PRO includes automated market scans, playbook rules, AI-assisted validation, paper-mode testing, supported broker-connected routing, risk-based position sizing, and dashboard monitoring with audit visibility.

Do I need a supported broker?

Yes for broker-connected routing workflows. You can use paper testing before live routing, and broker permissions stay under user control.

Can I use paper mode first?

Yes. Paper-mode testing is available and recommended before live use so you can validate how your playbook behaves.

Can I cancel?

Yes. You can manage or cancel your subscription from your account dashboard where available. Cancellation stops future renewals, and PRO access generally remains active through the paid billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

Fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period begins, except where required by law or expressly approved by XeanVI in writing. Contact admin@xeanvi.com for billing questions.

Who processes payments?

Payments are processed by Stripe. XeanVI does not store full credit card numbers.

Is XeanVI financial advice?

No. XeanVI is software for rule-based trading workflows and does not provide investment, financial, legal, or tax advice. Trading involves risk, including possible loss of capital.
