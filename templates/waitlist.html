XeanVI PRO FAQ

Is XeanVI a trading signal service?

XeanVI is not positioned as a simple signal feed. It is designed as an automated trading execution and workflow platform for traders who want to define, validate, and follow their own trading rules.

Does XeanVI guarantee profitable trades?

No. XeanVI does not guarantee profits, account growth, returns, or any specific trading outcome. Trading involves substantial risk, and users are responsible for their own trading decisions.

Is paper trading the main product?

No. Paper trading should be treated as a testing mode for validating rules and workflows. The core product is XeanVI PRO, a monthly platform for automated trading execution workflows.
