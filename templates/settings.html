Logic Exclusion Parameters
SYS_FILTERS

Hard-code sector exclusions. The engine will automatically drop these assets from the morning scan matrix.

// Ethical Sector Blocks
BLOCK_FOSSIL_FUELS
Exclude oil, gas, and coal exploration infrastructure.
BLOCK_WEAPONS_MFG
Exclude defense contractors and civilian firearm operations.
BLOCK_TOBACCO
Exclude nicotine and tobacco distribution assets.
// Volatility Mitigators
BLOCK_MICRO_CAP
Filter out highly volatile penny assets (Price < $5.00).
BLOCK_BIOTECH
Exclude biological research assets prone to severe FDA-related gaps.
