Broker Integration FAQs

Which brokers does XeanVI currently support?
XeanVI is designed to connect with supported broker APIs to facilitate automated trading workflows. Check your dashboard connections page to review the currently supported integrations and required account types.
Can XeanVI withdraw or transfer my funds?
No. XeanVI's supported broker API connections request trade-only execution capabilities. We do not have access to transfer or withdraw your capital. You should always review the exact permissions shown by your broker during the authorization phase.
Is this live automated trading?
Yes. With a PRO subscription and an authorized API connection, XeanVI acts as an automated execution engine. It will push live orders to your connected broker the moment market conditions match your strict playbook rules.
Should I test in paper trading before live trading?
Absolutely. We strongly advise that you test all playbook rules, risk parameters, and API automation settings in the paper sandbox before risking a single dollar of real capital in the live market.
