Operations Briefing

Common Logic Queries

Quick answers to the most frequent terminal questions. For deep technical specifications on our routing, visit our Documentation Page.

Do I just hit "Run Morning Scan" once a day?

It is best practice to run the scan shortly before the market open to identify the "Best Pick" based on pre-market volume. We recommend running the scanner again after 9:45 AM ET to authorize the engine once the initial opening volatility "WAIT" gate is lifted.

How does the AI decide to enter a trade?

The engine uses a strict Screen, Validate, Execute workflow. It utilizes Gemini-based sentiment scoring alongside hard quantitative metrics like Relative Volume (RVOL) and float size. Only when all parameters defined in your playbook align will it transmit an order.

Is my money safe during a "Faceless" setup?

Yes. XeanVI utilizes secure OAuth tokens through official brokerage APIs like Alpaca. We never have access to your login credentials or your funds. The execution bot only has permission to route trades strictly within the risk parameters you have authorized.
