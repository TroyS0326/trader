Common Logic Questions

Quick answers about the XeanVI workflow. For deeper technical details, visit the Features Page.

Should I start with paper trading?

Yes. Paper trading is the recommended starting point. It lets you review scan behavior, playbook rules, and workflow settings before deciding whether to connect supported live broker tools.

How does XeanVI use AI-assisted scoring?

XeanVI can use AI-assisted scoring to organize market context, catalyst language, and scan results. The score is a workflow signal only and should not be treated as financial advice or a guarantee that a trade will be profitable.

Does XeanVI control my broker account?

You remain responsible for your broker account, permissions, settings, and trading activity. XeanVI is designed to support broker-connected workflows based on settings you configure and permissions you authorize.
