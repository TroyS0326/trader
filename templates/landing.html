Operations Briefing

Common Logic Questions

Quick answers about the XeanVI workflow. For deeper technical details, visit the Features Page.

Is XeanVI automated after setup?

Yes. After you define a playbook, complete onboarding, connect a supported broker, and authorize required permissions, XeanVI can run scans and route orders based on your configured rules. You remain responsible for your broker account, permissions, and trading decisions.

Can I test the automation before using live capital?

Yes. Paper testing is recommended before live use so you can verify how your playbook, scan criteria, and risk settings behave in simulated conditions before routing with live capital.

Does XeanVI control my broker account?

Funds remain with your supported broker. You control broker permissions, and XeanVI only operates within the access you authorize. You remain responsible for account settings and trading decisions.

Is this financial advice?

No. XeanVI does not provide financial advice or guaranteed results. It is software that follows user-defined rules and workflows, and you remain responsible for each trading decision.
