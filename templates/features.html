PRO Features Details

What do I get with XeanVI PRO?
XeanVI PRO includes automated scans, playbook rules, AI-assisted validation, paper testing, broker-connected routing, and dashboard monitoring with audit visibility.
Is execution fully automated?
XeanVI can route order instructions after playbook, broker, risk, and permission requirements are configured and conditions pass. You remain responsible for all trading activity and decisions.
Can I test before live capital?
Yes. Paper testing is available and recommended before enabling live routing.
Does XeanVI hold my funds?
No. Funds remain with your supported broker, and you control broker permissions.
Is this financial advice?
No. XeanVI does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or guaranteed results.
