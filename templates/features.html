PRO Features FAQs

What exactly do I get with XeanVI PRO?
A PRO subscription unlocks rule-based automation tools. This includes automated market scans, AI-assisted setup review, and supported broker API connectivity to route orders after your playbook, broker, and risk settings are configured.
Is the order execution fully automated?
XeanVI supports automated order routing after your playbook, broker connection, and risk settings are configured. You define the rules and permissions, and the platform routes order instructions when those conditions are met.
Can I test my rules before risking live capital?
Absolutely. XeanVI includes a paper trading execution sandbox. We highly recommend testing your playbook logic and risk controls in paper mode to ensure the automation behaves as expected before engaging live PRO execution.
Does XeanVI hold my trading funds?
No. We do not hold or manage your capital. XeanVI PRO relies on a secure API connection to route instructions to your own supported brokerage account. You retain full control over your funds and access permissions.
