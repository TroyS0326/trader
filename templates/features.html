How the Bot Works

You don't need to know how to read stock charts. The AI handles the heavy lifting in three simple steps.

1

The AI News Scanner (Finds the Trade)

Before the market even opens, the AI scans thousands of financial news headlines. It calculates volume and social hype to find the exact stocks preparing to pop. Zero manual research required.

2

Market Crash Protection (Blocks Bad Trades)

Beginners lose money by buying right before the market drops. XeanVI actively monitors the overall stock market. If things are secretly dumping, the bot hits the brakes and refuses to buy, protecting your cash.

3

Smart Risk Sizing (Secures the Profit)

When the AI finds a safe trade, it takes over. It calculates exactly how much to buy so you never risk your whole account. It buys in, automatically takes the profit when the stock goes up, and gets out. Flawless execution.
