Platform Architecture FAQs

Do I need to know how to code to use these features?
No coding experience is required. XeanVI provides a visual, intuitive dashboard. You simply set your risk parameters and technical preferences inside your Trading Playbook, and the engine automatically translates those rules into executable code.
How fast does the automated execution trigger?
Extremely fast. Because XeanVI is hosted on dedicated VPS infrastructure, our web-socket connections stream directly to the broker. When your playbook conditions are met, order routing happens in milliseconds, bypassing the latency of human reaction time.
Can I test the features before risking real money?
Absolutely. Every account begins in our Paper Trading Sandbox. You have full access to the AI scanning and execution routing using simulated capital, allowing you to prove your strategy's edge before upgrading to a live account.
