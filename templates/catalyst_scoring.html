Proprietary ML Stack

The Intelligence Engine

XeanVI leverages specialized Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Gradient Boosting to quantify market catalysts with mathematical precision.

Layer 1: FinBERT-Tone Sentiment

Standard AI struggles with financial nuance. XeanVI utilizes yiyanghkust/finbert-tone, a model specifically fine-tuned on financial corpora to detect subtle tonal shifts in news and earnings.

  • Neural Sentiment Parsing: Categorizes headlines into Positive, Negative, or Neutral classes.
  • Net Alpha Scoring: Calculates sentiment as Prob(Positive) - Prob(Negative) to determine the true conviction of a catalyst.
  • Local Inference: Runs directly on the XeanVI stack to ensure sub-millisecond data availability for the 9:20 AM batch process.

Layer 2: XGBoost Probability Modeling

Sentiment is a signal, but context is the edge. Our XGBoost Classifier correlates sentiment with quantitative market data to generate a final setup grade.

Feature Vector A

Premarket RVOL

Calculates the ratio of current premarket volume against the 30-day average to detect institutional footprint.

Feature Vector B

Gap Exhaustion

Applies mathematical penalties to "exhaustion gaps" exceeding 15% to protect against buying the top.

Feature Vector C

P(Success) Score

Outputs a definitive probability score (0.01 to 0.99) that determines if the Alpaca execution engine is unlocked.
