Terminal Operations

The "WAIT" Protocol

The system includes a built-in safety gate. Even if a stock looks perfect, the engine blocks all buying until after 09:45 AM ET. This ensures the "Opening Range" is established and we aren't buying into a "gap and crap" scenario.

Execution Blocks

The engine will block trades if: the spread is too wide, the stock float is too high, or the VIX is spiking. These are non-negotiable safety filters.

Risk & Exits

Automated Bracket Orders

Every entry is paired with a Hard Stop, Target 1 (scale-out + move to breakeven), and Target 2 (trailing stop for runners).

Daily Loss Lock

Once your user-defined daily loss threshold is met, the engine terminates all scanning and execution for the session to prevent emotional revenge trading.
