Module_02 // AI & Execution Logic
The engine operates strictly on a three-phase quantitative workflow, removing human emotion entirely:
  • 01_SCREEN
    Data Filtration: The system scans the market for news catalysts, highly abnormal relative volume (RVOL), and tight bid/ask spreads.
  • 02_VALIDATE
    Sentiment & Tech: Uses the Gemini API to assign a 0-100 institutional sentiment score, and cross-references price action against the daily Volume Point of Control (POC).
  • 03_EXECUTE
    Risk Sizing: Calculates exact position sizing based on your predefined bankroll and the Kelly Criterion to mathematically prevent over-leveraging.
Module_03 // Risk Management
The system includes a built-in temporal safety gate. Even if a setup perfectly aligns with all technical parameters, the engine explicitly blocks buying execution until after 09:45 AM ET.

This mandatory waiting period ensures the "Opening Range" is fully established, protecting your capital from violent "gap and trap" volatility scenarios common at the market open.
Manual exit management is obsolete. The moment XeanVI executes an entry, it automatically attaches a complex bracket order to the trade via the broker API. This includes:

• Hard Stop: Absolute downside protection to cut losses immediately.
• Target 1: A scale-out point where the engine secures partial profits and automatically moves your stop-loss to breakeven.
• Target 2: A dynamic trailing stop that follows the asset higher to capture maximum upside on "runners."
XeanVI is a highly disciplined engine designed for capital preservation. It will ruthlessly block execution if environmental variables are unsafe. Common blocks include:

1. Slippage Risk: The spread between the Bid and Ask is too wide.
2. Insufficient Momentum: The stock's public float is too high to generate meaningful alpha.
3. Loss Lock: You have hit your pre-set "Daily Loss Lock" (maximum failed setups for the session).
4. Macro Volatility: The VIX (Volatility Index) is actively spiking, indicating a dangerous, unpredictable broader market environment.
