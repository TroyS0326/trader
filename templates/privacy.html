Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

XeanVI ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and financial information when you use our algorithmic trading software and website.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our algorithmic trading services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Data: Full legal name, email address, physical address, and phone number (collected during registration).
  • Brokerage & Financial Data: OAuth access tokens and account IDs used to securely connect to your third‑party brokerage (e.g., Alpaca). We do not directly collect or store your banking information or social security numbers.
  • Trading Data: Trading parameters (bankroll size, risk percentages), executed order history, and portfolio performance metrics necessary to run the algorithm.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, and interaction metrics with our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and secure the execution engine.
  • Route your selected trades securely to your linked brokerage API.
  • Calculate dynamic position sizing based on your defined risk parameters.
  • Communicate critical system updates, margin warnings, or account notices.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third‑party brokerages (like Alpaca) strictly to facilitate your requested trades.
  • Legal Compliance: If required to do so by law, subpoena, or regulatory authority (such as the SEC or FINRA).

5. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data. Brokerage API tokens are encrypted at rest using enterprise‑grade cryptography. Passwords are cryptographically hashed, and all web traffic is secured via SSL/TLS encryption.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you disconnect your brokerage account or delete your XeanVI profile, we will securely destroy your OAuth tokens and trading history, retaining only basic account records required for legal compliance.

7. Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding your privacy or data security, please contact our support team through your user dashboard.
