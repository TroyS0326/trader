Operations Briefing

Common Automation Questions

Quick answers about the XeanVI PRO live workflow. For deeper technical details, visit the Features Page.

Is this fully automated live trading?

Yes. Once you configure your playbook rules and link a supported broker API, XeanVI PRO will scan, score, and automatically route live trades based exclusively on your defined parameters. We always recommend testing in our sandbox paper mode before risking real capital.

How does risk management work?

You define your max risk, stop-loss percentages, and position sizing right inside your playbook. XeanVI PRO strictly enforces these risk controls on every automated execution, automatically calculating shares and attaching bracket orders to protect your account.

Do you require a specific broker?

No. XeanVI PRO integrates with supported broker APIs. You connect your existing account to authorize execution, and you maintain complete control over your capital and broker permissions at all times.
