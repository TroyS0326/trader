# XeanVI Meta Ad Launch Playbook — Buy 1 Month, Get 1 Month Free

## 1. Campaign Goal
Drive policy-safe, high-intent traffic from Meta to XeanVI's pricing and signup flow for a limited **Buy 1 Month, Get 1 Month Free** launch offer. The campaign should position XeanVI as trading workflow software focused on structure, paper-testing support, and execution discipline—not profit guarantees.

## 2. Hard Compliance Rules

### Do Not Use
- No guaranteed profit claims.
- No personal financial-attribute callouts.
- No “make money fast.”
- No “AI picks winning stocks.”
- No “automatic profits.”
- No “quit your job.”
- No “financial freedom.”
- No “bad credit,” “debt,” “broke,” or income-status language.
- No before/after trading results.
- No screenshots implying brokerage performance unless real, compliant, and heavily disclaimed.
- No testimonials claiming profit unless legally reviewed.

### Safe to Use
- Rule-based trading workflow.
- Paper trading.
- Trading playbook.
- Scanner validation.
- Bracket order workflow.
- Risk rules.
- Automation assistance.
- Structured execution.
- Limited launch offer.
- Software platform.
- Trading involves risk.

## 3. Recommended Campaign Setup
1. Open **Meta Business Suite** and go to **Ads Manager**.
2. Click **Create**.
3. Choose a campaign objective.
4. Recommended objective: **Sales** if Pixel purchase/checkout events are verified; otherwise start with **Leads** or **Traffic**.
5. Leave **Special Ad Category** off unless Meta explicitly requires it.
6. Campaign name format: `XeanVI | BOGO Month | Prospecting | [YYYY-MM-DD]`.
7. Buying type: keep default **Auction**.
8. Campaign budget option: turn **Advantage Campaign Budget** on for initial testing.
9. Advantage Campaign Budget recommendation: enable for simplified budget allocation across ad sets.
10. Start with a modest daily test budget (example: **$20–$50/day** depending on risk tolerance).
11. Keep A/B test off for first launch unless you are intentionally testing a single variable.
12. Move to **Ad Set** setup.
13. Conversion location: choose **Website**.
14. Performance goal: optimize for the deepest reliable event (Purchase/InitiateCheckout/LPV based on tracking quality).
15. Select Pixel and event if available.
16. Build audience.
17. Location targeting: **United States** unless owner chooses otherwise.
18. Age targeting: **25+** recommended.
19. Gender: **All**.
20. Detailed targeting: keep broad; avoid targeting based on financial stress or personal hardship attributes.
21. Safe interests to test only if needed:
   - Day trading
   - Stock market
   - Trading software
   - Technical analysis
   - Paper trading
   - Alpaca
   - Retail trading
   - Financial markets
   Broad audience often outperforms overly stacked interest combinations.
22. Placements: use **Advantage+ placements** for initial testing.
23. Optimization and delivery: align with your selected performance event.
24. Build ad creative.
25. Identity: select the **XeanVI Facebook Page** and connected Instagram account (if used).
26. Add destination URL.
27. Add UTM parameters.
28. Preview all placements and verify text overlays and cropping.
29. Click **Publish**.
30. Complete post-publish review checklist (Section 13).

## 9. Creative Direction

### A) Clean SaaS Dashboard Graphic
- Visual idea: modern UI-style mockup showing watchlist/scanner cards and rule checklist.
- Text overlay: “Rule-Based Trading Workflow”
- What NOT to include: profit graphs, account balances, unrealistic returns, luxury/lifestyle cues.
- Recommended image ratios: 1:1 and 4:5.
- Prompt: “Create a clean dark-mode SaaS dashboard mockup for trading workflow software with scanner cards, checklist widgets, and neutral UI metrics. Minimal text overlay: Rule-Based Trading Workflow. Premium, simple, trustworthy.”

### B) Playbook Workflow Graphic
- Visual idea: step sequence (Build → Test → Execute) with iconography and subtle grid background.
- Text overlay: “Build. Test. Execute.”
- What NOT to include: claims of winning trades, rocket/moon symbols, cash piles, brokerage P&L.
- Recommended image ratios: 1:1 and 4:5.
- Prompt: “Design a minimal workflow graphic for trading software showing three steps: Build, Test, Execute. Clean typography, neutral colors, no performance claims, professional SaaS branding style.”

### C) Offer-Focused Graphic
- Visual idea: bold central offer card with supporting line about structured workflow software.
- Text overlay: “Buy 1 Month, Get 1 Free”
- What NOT to include: countdown timers, fake scarcity, misleading urgency, financial distress cues.
- Recommended image ratios: 1:1 and 4:5.
- Prompt: “Create a premium promotional ad graphic for SaaS trading workflow platform featuring text Buy 1 Month, Get 1 Free and secondary message Rule-Based Trading Workflow. Clean layout, high contrast, minimal elements.”

## 14. Post-Publish Optimization Plan (First 72 Hours)
- Do not make major edits in the first few hours unless delivery is broken.
- Watch: CTR, CPC, Landing Page Views, signups, checkout starts, and purchase completion (if available).
- Pause only clear underperformers (very low CTR + high CPC + no downstream actions).
- Duplicate winning ads/ad sets instead of endlessly editing active winners.
- Test one variable at a time (headline, creative, or audience—not all at once).
